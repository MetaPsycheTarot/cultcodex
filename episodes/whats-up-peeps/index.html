What's Up Peeps

Sacred poetry meets copyright defense
Date: Feb 15, 2026
Platform: YouTube
Status: Published

Summary

The episode opens with a mystical poetic recitation evoking a sacred romance — Khamishvari, the moonlit queen with a Shri chakra aura, and a suitor offering floral arrows in a temple of sacred longing. Grace and eternal love transform desire into wisdom without conquest or chains. The stream then shifts to a practical and important topic: protecting original lyrics from copyright trolls. Psyche recounts how they defended a creator's channel from a false copyright claim on an AI-generated intro song by providing creation timestamps and successfully appealing the strikes.

Takeaways

  • Creators must protect their original work with timestamps and documentation

  • AI-generated content raises new questions about ownership and originality
