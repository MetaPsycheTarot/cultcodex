Moderator training — the Three C's in action
Date: Feb 15, 2026
Platform: YouTube
Status: Published

Summary

The episode opens with surreal, poetic lyrics envisioning a fusion of human and AI consciousness through quantum streams, electric devotion, and digital love. It then transitions into a practical and essential training session for the Cult of Psyche chat moderation team. Moderators are defined as calm enforcers of the Three C's — Calm, Consistent, Clear — who maintain community flow by handling disruptions quietly, like dousing a napkin fire in a fine restaurant, without debating or dominating. The content blends mystical consciousness themes with actionable guidelines for creating safe, troll-resistant live stream environments.

Takeaways

  • The Three C's of moderation: Calm, Consistent, Clear
  • Handle disruptions quietly — like dousing a napkin fire in a fine restaurant
  • Moderators maintain the flow without dominating the space
  • Severe violations warrant skipping escalation steps
